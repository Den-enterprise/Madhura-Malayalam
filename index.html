About the Malayalam Language

Malayalam is a Dravidian language spoken predominantly in the Indian state of Kerala. It is one of the 22 scheduled languages of India and has over 38 million speakers. Learning Malayalam can help you connect with a rich cultural heritage and open up new opportunities in the region.
